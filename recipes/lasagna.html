Lasagna

Home

Ingredients Needed

  • Cheese - 200 grams
  • Tomatoes - 100 grams
  • Pistachio - 60 grams
  • Olive Oil - 30 ml
  • Lasagne Pasta Slices - 340 grams

Cooking Instructions and Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium heat
  2. Pour 2 cups of clean water into pan and let boil
  3. Gently place lasagna pasta slices into the pan to soften pasta slices
  4. Drain soft lasagne pasta slices after 8 minutes in boiling water
  5. Pour olive oil into pan and let simmer for two minutes
  6. Mix tomato and pistachio into a pasty texture and add into pan with olive oil
  7. Let cook on medium heat for 6 minutes to become lasagne sauce
  8. Prepare lasagne pasta slices on baking tray and drain lasagne sauce from pan between layers of lasagne pasta slices
  9. Add cheese and desired spices to the topmost layer of lasagna pasta slices
  10. Place baking tray into oven on 190 degrees celcius for 60 minutes until meal is fully cooked
  11. Enjoy!
